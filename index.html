The Platform

Safety

  • Promote CPR/First Aid classes and provide additional AEDs around campus
  • Coordinate with OCSO to increase security for students living off campus
  • Provide additional emergency preparedness training
  • Continue to install cameras in parking garages
  • Promote Safe Ride program and ensure it is easily accessible
  • Install more lighting on campus

 

Resources

  • Provide additional resources to transfer and veteran students
  • Provide SGA support/resources to regional campuses
  • Expand variety of SARC tutoring resources and designate additional study areas
  • Improve classroom and Student Union room reservation system
  • Host joint diversity initiatives with RSOs, agencies, and SGA
  • Provide opportunity for students to register to vote at orientation

 

Representation

  • Solicit student input on issues through surveys
  • Hold UCF administration accountable
  • Reform and update the UCF Golden Rule
  • Create International, Transfer, & Veteran Student Affairs Coordinator cabinet position
  • Create Graduate Student Affairs Coordinator cabinet position
  • Create Fraternity and Sorority Affairs Coordinator cabinet position
  • Actively seek to fill all SGA positions

 

School Pride

  • Distribute more ducks during Spirit Splash
  • Transportation to spirit events from regional campuses
  • Protect student tailgating rights
  • T-shirt exchange program - Exchange T-shirts from other colleges for UCF T-shirts

 

Quality of Life

  • Strengthen communication with RWC to accommodate student needs
  • Oppose new construction without first adding sufficient parking
  • Improve bus schedule to and from Rosen campus
  • Increase quantity of phone charging stations on campus
  • Encourage local restaurants to provide student discounts
  • Knightro’s Suits - A donated suit/professional clothes rental program

 

Transparency

  • Require cabinet members to sign waiver to allow their names to be shown on all SGA documents
  • Continue platform tracker on SGA website
  • Host monthly town hall meetings
  • Host press conferences and increase contact with media
  • Provide timely email updates regarding SGA operations and finances

 

Advocacy

  • Advocate for students during ongoing Aramark/Student Union Expansion negotiations
  • Advocate for tax free textbooks and improved textbook affordability
  • Advocate for no increase in cost of tuition
  • Advocate for more transparent student conduct process
  • Advocate for the construction of Greek Park Two
  • Advocate for removal of required summer classes
  • Advocate for the elimination of the Fraternity and Sorority Moratorium
  • Advocate for the elimination of the excess credit hour surcharge

The Senate Voting Record

Jake Milich voted in favor of:

  • Extending Florida Bright Futures Scholarship eligibility to high school graduates who fulfill full-time religious or service obligations before attending college. (Senate Resolution 48-03)

  • Holding SGA Senators accountable by lowering the number of senate meetings they are allowed to miss before being removed from senate. (Senate Resolution 48-05)

  • Repealing the regulation which requires university students to earn 9 credit hours during summer semesters. (Senate Resolution 48-08)

  • Making textbooks and other instructional materials more affordable. (Senate Resolution 48-10)

  • Allocating SGA funds to RSOs more quickly by lowering the amount of time that the Senior Student Affairs Officer can wait to sign senate bills. (Constitutional Amendment 48-03)

  • Allocating SGA funds to RSOs more quickly by lowering the amount of time that the Student Body President can wait to sign senate bills. (Constitutional Amendment 48-04)

  • Sponsoring legislation to promote a more transparent SGA. (Click to read more.)

  • Advocating for students voices to be heard in the Aramark Corporation proposal. (Click to read more.)
